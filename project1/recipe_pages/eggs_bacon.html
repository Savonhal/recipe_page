INSTRUCTIONS

Pan fry the bacon: Fry bacon in a pan on medium-low flame until they are crispy. Transfer them into a plate.

Cook eggs and cherry tomatoes: Use the same pan to cook eggs. Crack eggs in the pan and cook them as you like; sunny side up. Cover the pan so that the egg cooks properly. Slice cherry tomatoes and cook in the pan at the same time..

Season the eggs and serve with bacon: Add seasoning and garnish with chopped fresh parsley. Serve bacon and eggs hot.
